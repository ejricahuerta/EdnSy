Stat defaults (if CSV missing)

Booking widget
The appointment panel on the right side of the hero is the highest-converting element. Always include it. Link to their existing booking page or a generic contact form â€” do not remove the panel even if no booking system is connected.
Services â€” max 6, show 3
Display exactly 3 service cards in the grid. If CSV has more services, pick the 3 most common: General Dentistry, Cosmetic, and one specialty. Remaining services listed as text below the grid.
Light theme rationale
Healthcare buyers â€” both patients and practice owners â€” respond to clean, light, trustworthy aesthetics. White surfaces and teal signal clinical calm without being cold. Never use dark themes for healthcare demos.
Tone rule
All copy should feel warm and human, not clinical. Avoid words like "procedure," "treatment plan," "protocol." Prefer "visit," "care," "appointment." The practice owner needs to feel the site speaks to their patients the way they do.

When to use this template

Dynamic fields
Replace [business_name], [programs], [classes], [phone], [email]. Stats: members, classes/week, rating. Service cards from CSV or defaults (Personal Training, Group Classes, Nutrition).
Vital teal rule
Teal #0d9488 is the primary CTA and accent. Use for buttons, section labels, stats in the dark bar. Lime #84cc16 only for optional secondary highlights â€” do not overuse.
Voice & tone
Energetic but clear. CTAs: "Start Free Trial", "View Programs", "Book a Session". Headlines should feel motivating and inclusive. Avoid corporate or stiff language.

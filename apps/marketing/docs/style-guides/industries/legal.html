05 ‚Äî Usage Notes for Lead Rosetta
When to use this template
Use for law firms, solo practitioners, and professional services (accounting, consulting) where the prospect list or business category indicates legal/professional. Match tone: authoritative, structured, trustworthy.
Dynamic fields
Replace [firm_name], [practice_areas], [years_exp], [phone], [email]. Practice pills and service cards from CSV or default list (Corporate, Litigation, Real Estate).
Forest green rule
Forest #2d4a3e is the primary CTA and accent. Use for buttons, section eyebrows, left-border on cards. Gold #b8860b only for subtle trust cues (e.g. ratings) ‚Äî do not overuse.
Voice & tone
Conservative, clear, confident. Avoid playful or casual copy. CTAs: "Schedule Consultation", "Book a Call", "Practice Areas". Headlines should feel professional and reassuring.

# Midday.ai homepage layout reference

Order and structure as of 2025 (from midday.ai fetch).

## 1. Hero
- **Background:** Dark (black/near-black)
- **Content:** Single column, no side card
  - Small eyebrow (e.g. "Run your business finances without manual work | Midday")
  - One large H1 (e.g. "For the new wave of one-person companies")
  - One sentence sub
  - Single primary CTA button
  - Small line under CTA (e.g. "14-day free trial · Cancel anytime")
- No product/dashboard card in hero; no second column.

## 2. How it works (section label only)
- Small heading/label: "How it works"
- No content block; leads into the feature sections below.

## 3–7. Feature blocks (one section per feature)
Each block has:
- **One big H2** (e.g. "All your transactions, unified", "Invoices get paid", "Reconciliation gets handled", "Understand what's happening", "Stay updated and in control")
- **One sentence** body
- **One large visual** (dashboard table, inbox list, chart, etc.)
Layout: H2 and body then visual (stacked), or text left / visual right. Alternating or same background per block.

## 8. How it works (nav/list)
- List of the 5 feature section titles (optional summary/nav).

## 9. "Everything you need to run your business"
- **H2:** "Everything you need to run your business"
- **One sentence** body
- **Grid of feature cards** – each card: title + link (e.g. Assistant, Insights, Transactions, Inbox, Time tracking, Invoicing, Customers, Files). 8 items on Midday.

## 10. "Less admin. More focus."
- **H2:** "Less admin. More focus."
- **One sentence** body
- **Cards:** Each card = problem label (e.g. "Chasing receipts") → **big metric** ("45 minutes per week") → short description. One final CTA card (e.g. "Get your time back" / "4–6 hours").

## 11. "Listen to your weekly summary"
- Audio/special section (optional for other sites).

## 12. "Ready for accounting, without extra work"
- H2 + body + link (optional for other sites).

## 13. "Works with the tools you already use"
- **H2:** "Works with the tools you already use"
- **One sentence** (e.g. "Connect your banks, email, payments and accounting software in minutes.")
- **Integration logos** (grid or strip).

## 14. Pricing
- Pricing section. Other sites may use a CTA block instead.
